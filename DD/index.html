DungeonDraft Asset Hacks v1.3
Below, you will find the textual representation of custom assets (layered objects) made in Dungeondraft and then pasted here as text. To import them into your own Dungeondraft session. Please feel free to let me know if you have any questions about this document or want to add anything morvoldpress@yahoo.com.

Thank! Jeff @ Morvold Press

How to:

  1. Have Dungeondraft open.
  2. Find the Asset that you want on this page.
  3. Click "Copy all text" button
  4. Go into Dungeondraft and click the “Select Tool” (the arrow icon at the bottom of the tool menu on the left side)
  5. Paste
  6. It should be that easy – the asset should appear!
